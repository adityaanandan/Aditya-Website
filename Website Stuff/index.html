Projects

See My Work

I've always been interested in coding and programming. Over the past few years, I've created a bunch of projects which involve gaming, data analysis, and cybersecurity. I mainly focus on Java, Python, and Unreal Engine, but I've also dabbled in some web design and even crypto!

Over the course of my high school career, I've grown to view coding not as a chore, but as something I love to do. I've learned multiple programming languages, and built these projects as a means to express myself.


Books

Check out My Book!

My love for programming has also extended to writing. My journey with CS wasn't easy, and I only grew to love the subject through building games and projects. Therefore, through my published book: "Leveling Up: An Interactive Guide to Programming and Game Design", I hope to simplify and make CS more fun!

"Leveling Up" consists of three levels, each with a higher difficulty level. Each level will contain 5-7 projects that will convey programming or game design concepts. Click the link above to see my book on GitBook, or buy the full version on Amazon!


Podcast

From a young age, I've always loved

Brown Boys is my podcast where my
