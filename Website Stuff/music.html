Konakkol




I have been learning the rythmic side
of Carnatic vocal, Konnakol, for the past
7 years. Konakkol is deeply associated
with the instrument mridangam, and I
have been been performing with the
guidance of my teacher.

Vocal




Indian Classical vocal has been an
integral part of my childhood, as I
have been learning for the past 12
years. I have performed extensively
both in the US and abroad, and
hope to continue this passion
into college.

Violin




I started learning violin at the
age of 5, and continued until
2018. I have since
shifted my focus to vocal and Konakkol.
I still occassionally
play for fun on my brother's violin :)
